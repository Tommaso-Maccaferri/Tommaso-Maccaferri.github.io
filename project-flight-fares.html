Flight Fares Price Variation Analysis

Course: Programming Lab I | Institution: University of Bologna

Statistical analysis of pricing patterns in the airline industry, examining factors influencing fare variations across routes, seasons, and booking times.

Project Focus

This study focuses on identifying the main drivers of airline ticket pricing. By processing large datasets of historical flight fares, the project explores how distance, demand seasonality, and the timing of the booking affect the final price offered to consumers.

Technical Implementation

The analysis leverages quantitative methods to visualize trends and test hypotheses regarding price discrimination and market competition. Special attention was given to data cleaning and the creation of intuitive visualizations to represent complex pricing dynamics.

Tools: R | Methods: Regression analysis, Data visualization.
