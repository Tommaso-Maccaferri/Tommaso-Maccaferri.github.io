Investigating the correlation between Tertiary Education and Life Expectancy

Course: Programming Lab II | Institution: University of Bologna

Cross-country empirical study examining the relationship between higher education attainment and population health outcomes globally.

Key Insights

The research explores how institutional educational frameworks correlate with long-term public health metrics. By analyzing diverse global datasets, the study identifies patterns that link schooling years to increased longevity.

Methodology

The analysis focuses on the robustness of these correlations, accounting for regional disparities and economic development levels to ensure a balanced comparative perspective.

Tools: Stata | Methods: Cross-sectional regression, Correlation analysis.
