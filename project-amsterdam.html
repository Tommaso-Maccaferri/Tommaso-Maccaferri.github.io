Amsterdam’s Circular Economy Plan

Course: Public Management | Institution: University of Bologna

A strategic evaluation of Amsterdam’s transition to a circular economy, assessing stakeholder engagement and urban sustainability planning.

Project Overview

This study provides a strategic planning analysis of Amsterdam’s ambitious circular economy initiatives. The core objective was to critically evaluate the long-term feasibility of the city's environmental policies and its transition away from a linear economic model towards sustainable urban development.

Technical Approach

The analysis extensively utilizes Bryson’s Strategic Planning Framework to map out stakeholder engagement, identify key strategic issues, and assess policy effectiveness. The report breaks down the complex network of public and private actors required to execute municipal sustainability goals at scale.

Skills: Strategic Planning, Bryson Model, Public Policy Evaluation, Stakeholder Management.
