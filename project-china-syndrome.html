Revaluation of “The China Syndrome”

Course: Empirical Econometrics I | Grade: 8.5/10 | Institution: Maastricht University

A comprehensive replication and critical revaluation of Autor, Dorn, and Hanson’s seminal 2013 paper on the impact of Chinese import competition on U.S. local labor markets.

Key Contributions

  • Fully replicated the original identification strategy, converting 1,500+ lines of STATA code into R.
  • Implemented exposure-robust standard errors following Borusyak et al. (2022).
  • Critically assessed instrument relevance (F-statistics > 100).

Impact

Findings aligned with ongoing debates on shift-share identification and exposure-based inference in trade literature. Validated key coefficients 12 years after original publication.

Tools: R (Tidyverse, AER), IV estimation, Panel Data.
