Strategy & Management Case Studies

Focus: Strategic Analysis, Management & Finance | Level: Bachelor's Degree

A collection of research papers and business cases focusing on strategic planning, organizational efficiency, and corporate financial health.

Amsterdam’s Circular Economy Plan

A strategic planning analysis of Amsterdam’s circular economy initiatives. The study utilizes Bryson’s framework to evaluate stakeholder engagement, environmental policy effectiveness, and the long-term feasibility of urban sustainability goals.

McDonald’s Functional Analysis

An in-depth organizational analysis of one of the world's most successful franchises. This study examines core business functions, the evolution of operational efficiency through standardization, and McDonald's strategic positioning in a changing global market.

Cairn Homes PLC Financial Analysis

A corporate finance study applying valuation principles to Cairn Homes PLC. The project involves a rigorous analysis of financial statements, annual reporting data, and market trends to assess the company’s financial health and growth potential.

Skills: Strategic Planning, Financial Modeling, Stakeholder Management.
