McDonald’s Functional Analysis

Course: Principles of Management 1 | Institution: Technological University Dublin (TUD)

An in-depth dive into the organizational architecture and strategic management of one of the world's most successful global franchises.

Project Overview

This project unpacks the corporate structure of McDonald's, focusing on how the multinational giant balances global standardization with local market adaptation (glocalization). It examines the core business functions that drive its operational supremacy.

Technical Approach

Through comprehensive organizational analysis, the report evaluates McDonald's supply chain logistics, human resource management, and marketing strategies. It highlights how efficiency is maintained across thousands of distinct international locations through rigid operational frameworks and continuous innovation.

Skills: Organizational Design, Corporate Strategy, Functional Analysis, Operations Management.
